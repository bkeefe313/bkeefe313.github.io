Musical Projects

I pursue music in a capacity I would call casual, but not unseriously. I think music is a vital part of my identity. I have spent more of my life playing an instrument than not. I spend a serious amount of my time exploring and engaging with new music.

Right now, the way I play music is spontaneous and unrecorded. But I wanted to set up this page with the intention of placing musical projects (songs or otherwise) here, and to express that this is part of who I am.
